About Us

Rooted in Tradition,
Woven with Pride

We are a small Filipino business passionate about preserving the art of native fiber weaving. Every rope and placemat we craft is a story — of land, of skill, and of love for our culture.

Using sustainably harvested abaca and lampakanay (cattail) plants, our products are made the traditional way — by hand, with care, and with pride in every twist and weave.
